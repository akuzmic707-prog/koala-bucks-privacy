Our app may collect the following types of data:

We use collected data for the following purposes:

Koala Bucks Games uses third-party tools that may collect non-personal data, such as:

We do not sell, trade, or rent users' personal identification information to others. Limited data may be shared with trusted third-party service providers solely for the purposes mentioned above (e.g., analytics, ads, or crash reporting).
